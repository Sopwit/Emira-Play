Emira

Privacy Policy

Emira is a Telegram Mini App built during Build on Stellar Bootcamp in Yozgat. This page explains what data may be used when you access the app.

Telegram Data

Emira may use limited Telegram account information, such as your Telegram user ID, username, and basic profile information, to authenticate your session and provide access to the Mini App.

Wallet Data

If you choose to connect a wallet, your public wallet address may be used to enable app functionality and blockchain-related features. Emira does not collect or store private keys or seed phrases.

Usage Data

Limited technical and usage data may be processed to support app functionality, stability, security, and product improvement.

How Data Is Used

  • To authenticate access to the Telegram Mini App
  • To provide wallet-related app features
  • To improve reliability, security, and user experience

Data Sharing

Emira does not sell personal data. Information is only used to operate and improve the app experience.
